The Problem We Found

Educational institutions in Saudi Arabia, and beyond, often face:

  • Static Assessments: Traditional tools evaluate performance without linking results to program objectives or broader learning goals.
  • Lack of Actionable Insights: Results alone don’t uncover why students are under performing whether due to ineffective curricula, teaching challenges, or resource gaps.
  • Missed Opportunities for Growth: Without real-time feedback, the system remains rigid, misaligned with evolving needs and the ambitions of Vision 2030.

To address these gaps, IES developed an AI-driven Assessment and Quality Assurance Platform. Built with cutting-edge technology and designed specifically for Saudi Arabia’s unique needs, our solution empowers institutions to take control of their outcomes.

Key Features of Our Platform

  • Real-Time Feedback: Empowering educators with immediate, data-rich insights to monitor progress and make informed interventions.
  • Learning Gap Analysis: Leveraging AI to uncover patterns in student struggles across topics, curricula, or instructional methods.
  • Dynamic Adaptation: Enabling continuous updates to teaching strategies and curricula based on real-time data.
  • Cheating Detection: Advanced AI ensures the integrity of assessments through both online and offline monitoring.
  • Centralized Data Architecture: Seamlessly integrates exam results, learning tracks, and question bank workflows, simplifying analysis and reporting.

Our Product

Product Overview & Competing Features

Systematic Learning Pathway Visualization and Mapping

Our platform offers a groundbreaking approach to aligning learning tracks with intended outcomes. Educators can visually map each learner's journey, ensuring every activity, topic, and exam is purposefully connected to achieving specific educational goals. This feature brings clarity and coherence to the entire learning process, driving focus and measurable progress.

Comprehensive Question Bank Management

Designed for efficiency and flexibility, our system allows institutions to build and manage extensive question banks. Educators can classify, organize, and customize questions by difficulty, subject, or criteria, ensuring assessments meet the precise needs of their curricula. Workflow tools streamline the process, saving valuable time for teachers and administrators.

Group Exam Administration & Assignment

Our solution simplifies the complexities of managing large-scale exams. Institutions can assign examinees, create groups, and oversee examinations seamlessly through a centralized interface. This capability enhances organization, minimizes logistical challenges, and ensures a smooth assessment experience.

AI-Powered Cheating Detection

Integrity in assessments is critical. Our AI-driven proctoring system uses cutting-edge algorithms to monitor both online and offline exams without human interference. This feature ensures secure and fair evaluation processes, protecting the credibility of educational and corporate certifications.

AI-Driven Exam Generation

Move beyond static tests with dynamic, AI-generated exams tailored to individual needs. Whether based on specific learning objectives, student performance history, or curriculum alignment, our platform produces intelligent, adaptive assessments that provide a deeper understanding of knowledge retention and application.

AI-Enhanced Curriculum Insights

Go beyond the numbers—our platform identifies patterns in student failures and connects them to specific subjects, learning objectives, or instructional methods. These insights are invaluable for refining curricula, improving instructional quality, and optimizing the entire educational experience.

Actionable Feedback and Analytics

Our system doesn’t just stop at grading. It provides detailed feedback and robust analytics on exam performance, helping educators identify trends and problem areas across individual students, groups, and entire institutions. These insights enable targeted interventions and informed decision-making.

Seamless Integration and Scalability

Whether you’re a small school or a national education body, our modular platform adapts to your needs. It integrates effortlessly with existing systems, scales to accommodate growth, and supports institutions of all sizes with the same high level of efficiency and effectiveness.
